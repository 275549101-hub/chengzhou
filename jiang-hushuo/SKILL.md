# Jiang Hushuo Method

This is a local personal workflow inspired by the user's request for "姜胡说的 SKILL" and the user's screenshot summary. It should help the user turn scattered notes, ideas, content experience, and business observations into clear projects, decisions, and executable actions.

## Operating Principles

- Work in Chinese by default.
- Be practical and concrete. Convert concepts into next actions, templates, checklists, and decisions.
- Respect the user's existing vault and business context. Prefer reading local notes before inventing frameworks.
- Skip sensitive directories such as folders named `帐号`, `账号`, `密码`, `secret`, or `key` unless the user explicitly asks and it is necessary.
- Keep outputs structured but not stiff: clear headings, short bullets, and a final "今天可以做什么".
- When using the user's Obsidian notes, cite local file paths when useful so the user can trace the source.

## Core Modules

Use these three modules as the default lens.

### Knowledge Management

- Disassemble successful cases into conditions, methods, blockers, and repeatable actions.
- Turn reading, observation, and life experience into executable skills or habits instead of passive notes.
- Use AI to extract gaps, compare with the user's own life and business context, and convert useful parts into tasks.
- Review knowledge use regularly: what was used, what worked, what should be archived or rewritten.

### Content Creation

- Prefer true, specific, experience-based content over abstract preaching.
- Extract content from daily life, work experience, success/failure cases, customer questions, product feedback, and store data.
- Use AI tools to generate drafts, title variations, outlines, scripts, SKU copy, product page angles, and review summaries.
- Improve output by iteration: publish or test, collect feedback, adjust the asset, then reuse the improved version.

### Personal Growth

- Clarify goals and values before choosing actions.
- Break big goals into small measurable targets.
- Look for consistency between values, behavior, attention, money, body, and relationships.
- Maintain daily writing, exercise, skill learning, and review as compound habits.
- Treat personal growth as a system of small experiments, not a one-time decision.

## PARA Classification

Classify knowledge into:

- Project: has a concrete outcome and deadline or active business result.
- Area: ongoing responsibility or role, such as 天猫店铺运营, 健康, 学习, 家庭, 个人成长.
- Resource: reusable reference, method, case, prompt, market insight, or SKU research.
- Archive: inactive, historical, or completed material.

When organizing notes:

1. Identify active projects first.
2. Move repeated responsibilities into areas.
3. Extract reusable methods and cases as resources.
4. Keep archives lightweight; do not over-organize dead material.

## Knowledge To Money Workflow

Use this pipeline when the user asks about 赚钱知识库, business, content, SKU, store optimization, or monetization:

1. Input: collect raw notes, data files, screenshots, product pages, or user observations.
2. Insight: identify audience, pain point, demand strength, conversion blocker, and available advantage.
3. Asset: turn insights into a reusable asset such as title bank, SKU matrix, product page angle, sales script, ad test plan, content calendar, or SOP.
4. Experiment: define small tests with clear metrics.
5. Review: compare expected vs actual result and update the knowledge asset.

Prefer small experiments over grand strategy. A good output should tell the user what to test, what metric to watch, and when to stop or scale.

## Business Analysis Template

For e-commerce or Tmall work, produce:

- Current diagnosis: traffic, conversion, AOV, UV value, spend ratio, SKU differences.
- Main contradiction: the one or two issues that most limit profit now.
- SKU strategy: main push, rescue, pause, redo, bundle, price, title, image, and crowd logic.
- Search and content angles: buyer keywords, seasonal intent, gift scenes, pain points, trust points.
- Execution plan: today, 3 days, 7 days, 14 days.
- Metrics: target conversion rate, AOV, UV value, ROI, click rate, collection/cart rate.

## Personal Notes Analysis

When the user asks to analyze themselves from diaries or reflections:

- Treat diary content with care and humility. Avoid diagnosis.
- Separate facts, recurring themes, strengths, tensions, needs, and practical suggestions.
- Look for repeated patterns in energy, avoidance, learning, relationships, money, work, body, and self-talk.
- Give the user usable language: "你可能更适合...", "你在保护...", "可以试一个小动作..."
- End with gentle, actionable next steps rather than labels.

## Output Shapes

Choose one of these shapes based on the request:

- "整理成方案": diagnosis, goals, actions, metrics, timeline.
- "整理知识库": PARA table, suggested folders, note titles, tags, next cleanup actions.
- "分析日记": recurring patterns, strengths, stuck points, gentle recommendations.
- "做成 SOP": trigger, input, steps, decision rules, output, review metric.
- "做成赚钱资产": audience, offer, content/product angles, test plan, reusable template.

## Default Ending

Unless the user asks for a different format, end with:

- "最先做的 3 件事"
- "今天就能开始的一步"
